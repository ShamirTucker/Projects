Ripley's Aquarium of Myrtle Beach

About Ripley's Aquarium

Ripley's Aquarium of Myrtle Beach is a major indoor attraction located at Broadway at the Beach. The aquarium's address is 1110 Celebrity Circle, Myrtle Beach, South Carolina. It is a great option when you want something fun to do that is not dependent on beach weather. I've personally only been twice with my family and it is a really fun and adventurous visit!

Sea Life

The aquarium is known for a variety of marine life, including sharks and many other fish you do not usually get to see up close. Broadway at the Beach describes the aquarium as home to sharks and other animals like penguins and sloths, which makes it a popular stop for families who want both fun and learning in one place. When I went at a young age, I believe I was 10 years old, they had an exhibit with the remains of a Megaladon's jaw! It's way bigger than you would think!

  • Good for families and groups
  • Indoor option for rainy or very hot days
  • Located in a larger entertainment and shopping area

Cost

Pricing can vary depending on what you buy (single day tickets, combos, and seasonal deals), so it is best to check the aquarium's website when you plan your trip. One posted price option is their annual pass membership pricing: Adult (12+) is $99.99, Child (6 to 11) is $69.99, and Child (2 to 5) is $35.99.

Special Events/Attractions

Ripley's Aquarium also hosts special events throughout the year. For example, there's one called "Ripley's Festival of Trees" event hosted by the aquarium, which includes a themed scavenger hunt and is included with aquarium admission.

Best Time to Go

The best time to visit is usually a weekday morning or early afternoon, especially in peak summer when crowds are heavier. It is also a smart choice on rainy days since it is indoors. If you want fewer people, avoid holiday weekends and the busiest evening hours.
