History of Myrtle Beach

Founding

Myrtle Beach did not start as a big city. The modern story really takes off in the early 1900s when the beachfront began being developed as a resort destination. One major early milestone is the Seaside Inn which was built in 1901, and it helped establish Myrtle Beach as a place people traveled to for vacation. The city name also came from local wax myrtle trees, and it later grew into a formal town and then a city as the resort community expanded.

Humble Beginnings

Before Myrtle Beach became the busy tourist destination it is now, the coast was described as mostly undeveloped and difficult to access. Once the resort development began, it would have felt more like a small beach town with simple lodging, fishing, and beach activities, not the huge entertainment destination it is today.

Military Involvement

Myrtle Beach became significant beyond tourism because of its military connection. The Myrtle Beach Air Force Base played a major role in the area for decades and influenced local growth, jobs, housing, and infrastructure. Even after the base closed, its impact is still part of the areas modern development.

Vibes of Myrtle Today

Today Myrtle Beach is a major vacation hub on the Grand Strand. It is built around tourism, so the area has beaches, hotels, restaurants, entertainment, and attractions that are designed for visitors. What started as a small resort community turned into a well-known coastal destination that stays busy throughout peak travel seasons.

Quick timeline

  • Early 1900s: Resort development begins; Seaside Inn (1901) is an early milestone.
  • Mid 1900s: Growth continues through tourism and major changes along the coast.
  • Late 1900s: The area continues expanding after major shifts like military base closure.
