Family Kingdom Amusement Park

What is it?

Family Kingdom is a seaside amusement park in Myrtle Beach. It is known for classic amusement park rides and the fact that it is very close to the ocean, so it feels like a beach day and an amusement park day in one trip.

When was it built?

Family Kingdom has been part of Myrtle Beach for decades. One of its most well-known rides is the Swamp Fox, a wooden roller coaster that the park highlights as a Myrtle Beach landmark since 1966.

This is yet another place in Myrtle Beach I have yet to visit. There's so much to do that it's hard to do everything.

Cost

Pricing depends on what you choose. Family Kingdom lists an "All Day Unlimited Ride Wristband" for $45.00, and they also list "Early Bird" pricing at $30.00 for a limited time window. These prices can change, so it is best to check their current General Info page before you go.

What is it most known for?

The park is most known for being a classic seaside amusement park, and for the Swamp Fox wooden coaster. It is said that if you want something that feels like old-school Myrtle Beach, this is one of the most iconic places to go.

Special Events

Family Kingdom runs seasonal specials and promotions, including Early Bird wristband deals. Depending on the time of year, they may also offer local and military discounts.

Best Time to Go

The best time to go is usually a weekday or earlier in the day before crowds build up. If you want the best value, Early Bird deals are a good option. If you want shorter lines, avoid peak summer nights and holiday weekends.
