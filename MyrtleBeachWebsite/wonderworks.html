WonderWorks Myrtle Beach

What is WonderWorks?

WonderWorks is an indoor attraction that mixes education and entertainment. It is known as an "amusement park for the mind" with more than 100 hands-on exhibits, and it is designed to feel like you are walking through an upside-down building. WonderWorks Myrtle Beach is located at Broadway at the Beach at 1313 Celebrity Circle, Myrtle Beach, South Carolina.

I haven't personally visited here either, but I visited a similar place in Charlotte, NC, so I know I would absolutely love this place and added it to my "to-go" list.

When was it built?

WonderWorks became one of the newer attractions added to Broadway at the Beach in 2011. That is when it was promoted as a new attraction coming to the complex.

Cost

Ticket prices can change, so it is best to verify on the official ticket page before going. One posted set of rates lists Adult (13-59) as $33.99 plus tax, Child (5-12) as $26.99 plus tax, and Senior (60+) as $26.99 plus tax. The ticket page also notes that ages 4 and under are free.

What is it most known for?

WonderWorks is most known for its upside-down building look and its interactive exhibits. It is designed so you can learn by doing, not just by reading. Visitors typically go for the hands-on science and the unique photo opportunities, especially at exhibits like the bed of nails.

Special Events/Attractions

WonderWorks has multiple add-ons and experiences that are part of the attraction, depending on what ticket you buy. The official site describes features like a glow-in-the-dark indoor ropes course, a laser tag arena, and an XD theater style motion experience. They also offer online purchase options and warn that peak seasons and rainy days can increase wait times.

Best Time to Go

The best time to go is usually a weekday morning or early afternoon, especially in peak season. It is also a great backup plan when it is raining or extremely hot outside, since everything is indoors. If you want a smoother visit, try to avoid holiday weekends and the busiest evening hours.
