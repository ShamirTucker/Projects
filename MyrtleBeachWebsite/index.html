Welcome to Myrtle Beach!

Where is Myrtle Beach?

Myrtle Beach is a coastal resort city on South Carolina's Grand Strand. It is known for wide beaches, a busy boardwalk, and lots of attractions built around tourism. I have been there lots of times throughout my life as I have lots of family in North and South Carolina. My family usually pays a visit when we go down South. It's really a great place to go and there's lots to do.

Use the map above to see where it sits along the Atlantic Ocean and how close it is to nearby cities and beach towns.

What you can do here

Myrtle Beach is built for vacation activities: beach days, piers, mini golf, shopping, and indoor attractions for rainy days. There's something to do there for everyone, whether you're an energetic athletic or more of a laid back person, there will definitely be something for you! Oh, did you know, it's also known as the Mini-Golf capital of the world?!
