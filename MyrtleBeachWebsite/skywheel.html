SkyWheel Myrtle Beach

When was it built?

The SkyWheel opened on May 20, 2011. It is located at 1110 N Ocean Blvd, Myrtle Beach, South Carolina, right on the Myrtle Beach Boardwalk.

What is it known for?

The SkyWheel is known for its ocean views and skyline views. It is an observation wheel with enclosed gondolas, so you can ride during the day, at sunset, or at night. It is a popular spot for photos because it stands out on the boardwalk and you can see far down the coastline from the top. I've unfortunately only been on it once when I was younger and don't have the strongest memory of it. Some things just get lost in time.

Quick Ride Facts

  • A regular flight is 4 revolutions and lasts about 10 to 15 minutes.
  • Each gondola can fit up to 6 adults comfortably.
  • Gondolas are heated and cooled for comfort.
  • Your ride is private with your group (they do not combine strangers).

Cost to Ride

Ticket prices (plus tax) are listed as $19.99 for adults and $15.99 for children ages 3 to 11. Ages 2 and under are free. They also list optional add-ons like VIP tickets and annual passes. Since pricing can change, it is smart to double check the official site before you go.
